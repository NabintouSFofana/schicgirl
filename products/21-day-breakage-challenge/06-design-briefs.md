# Design Briefs (Canva-ready)

## SHARED BRAND KIT
*Apply to all assets — this is what makes them one family.*

**Color palette (from the live Schicgirl site):**
- Cream (background) — `#FDF8F2`
- Warm sand (cards) — `#F5E5D5`
- Brown ink (primary text) — `#2D1A0E`
- Brown mid (subheads) — `#6B3E2E`
- Gold (accent/hero) — `#C9934A`
- Gold light (gradient partner) — `#E8C07E`
- Rose (warmth accent) — `#E8B4A0`
- Rose deep (CTA/emphasis) — `#C47A65`
- Success green (tiny "kept length" accent) — `#3A6B4C`
- Danger clay (tiny "breakage" accent) — `#8A3A33`

**Signature texture:** soft warm radial glows on cream — rose top-left, gold bottom-right, fading
`#FDF6EE → #F8ECE0`. Use faintly behind every asset.

**Font pairings (pick ONE per asset, don't mix):**
- Pairing A — Editorial: Headlines **Playfair Display** / Body **Mulish** (or Lato)
- Pairing B — Modern premium: Headlines **Fraunces** / Body **Mulish**
- Use A for sales/hero/graduation; B as fallback. Always one serif + one sans.

**Consistency rule:** warm cream/sand base + ONE gold OR rose-deep focal accent + one serif
headline + generous whitespace. One focal point per asset. Never more than two accent colors at
once. Green/clay only as tiny data accents.

**Copy lock:** the promised win is *less breakage + a routine that sticks* — never "longer hair in
21 days." Any length language is framed as "the length you keep."

## 1. Sales Page Hero Banner
- **Size:** 1920×900 px (also export 1080×1080 crop for social)
- **Layout:** Split editorial — left 55% text lockup on cream+glow; right 45% full-bleed portrait
  of a confident Type 4 woman, hand in her coils; thin gold hairline rule between panels.
- **Type:** Eyebrow "A SCHICGIRL CHALLENGE" (Mulish 18px, gold); Headline "Stop the Breakage"
  (Playfair 72px, brown); Subhead "21-Day Length Retention Challenge" (Playfair Italic 38px, gold);
  Promise "Less breakage. A routine that finally sticks." (Mulish 22px); CTA on rose-deep.
- **Message:** This challenge stops your breakage and builds a routine that lasts.

## 2. The Reframe Graphic
- **Size:** 1080×1080 px
- **Layout:** Centered equation, huge whitespace. "Growth (root) − Breakage (ends) = the length you keep."
- **Color:** Growth in green `#3A6B4C`; Breakage in clay `#8A3A33`; result in gold `#C9934A` (largest).
- **Type:** Terms Fraunces 40px; result Fraunces Bold 60px gold; footnote "You can't speed up
  growth. You CAN stop the loss." (Mulish 16px).
- **Message:** Length is what you retain — close the leak at the ends.

## 3. Mockup Set (cover + interior spread + tracker)
- **Sizes:** Cover 1080×1350; Interior spread 1600×1000; Tracker page 1275×1650 (print-ready).
- **Cover:** Title block on warm sand, gold rule, coil-texture band. **Interior:** left "How to use",
  right a Day template. **Tracker:** photo-checkpoint slots Day 7/14/21 (dashed gold frames) +
  comb-test boxes Day 1 vs 21; footer "Same light. Same section. Compare honestly."
- **Message:** A guided, trackable system — you see progress in your own photos and comb tests.

## 4. Broken vs Shed Strand Diagram
- **Size:** 1080×1080 px
- **Layout:** Two strands side by side. Left BROKEN: short jagged fragment, frayed both ends, no
  bulb. Right SHED: full-length strand with white bulb at root. Zoom callouts on each end.
- **Color:** Strands brown ink; broken label clay; shed label + bulb green; gold pointers.
- **Callouts:** "No bulb. Frayed. = damage to fix." / "White bulb. Full length. = natural cycle."
- **Message:** Broken (no bulb) = breakage; shed (white bulb) = normal.

## 5. Detangle Technique Mini-Graphic
- **Size:** 1080×1350 px
- **Layout:** Vertical 4-step flow, numbered gold circles. Banner: "Damp — not soaked. Loaded with
  conditioner slip." Steps: 1) Section into 4 · 2) Finger-detangle first · 3) Wide-tooth comb next
  · 4) Always ends → roots (arrows curving upward).
- **Message:** Detangle damp, in sections, fingers-then-comb, ends-to-roots — that's where breakage stops.

## 6. 3-Week Journey Strip
- **Size:** 1600×600 px (sliceable into 3× 1080×1080)
- **Layout:** Three panels, gold progress line + dots filling left-to-right. Week 1 "See it" / Week 2
  "Stop it" / Week 3 "Keep it"; tints warm rose → gold-light → gold.
- **Outcomes:** "Spot where you're losing length." / "Fix the habits causing breakage." / "Lock in
  a routine that sticks."
- **Message:** In three weeks you go from seeing the breakage to keeping your length.
