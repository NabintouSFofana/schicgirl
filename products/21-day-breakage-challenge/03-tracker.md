**The unbroken chain:** ▢▢▢▢▢▢▢ ▢▢▢▢▢▢▢ ▢▢▢▢▢▢▢ (fill one box per day)

**Milestones:** ⭐ Day 7 · ⭐ Day 14 · ⭐ Day 21 (wash-day breakage checks)

**Spotting cue (so you log accurately):**
> White bulb on the end + full-length strand = natural **shed** (normal).
> No bulb + short broken-off piece = **breakage** (what this challenge reduces).
