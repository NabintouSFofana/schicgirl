# Schicgirl — 90-Day SEO Plan (beginner, week by week)

Do the boxes in order. ~2–4 hours/week is enough. The goal of 90 days isn't "rank #1"
— it's to get indexed, publish a few keyword-targeted posts, and earn your first
backlinks so rankings *start* climbing. See [KEYWORDS-STARTER.md](KEYWORDS-STARTER.md)
for which keyword to use each week.

---

## MONTH 1 — Foundations (get found + learn the rhythm)

### Week 1 — Get indexed
- [ ] Set up **Google Search Console** (URL prefix `https://schicgirl.me`, verify).
- [ ] Submit sitemap: `sitemap.xml`.
- [ ] **Request indexing** for 5 pages: homepage, `/fr/pellicules/`, `/en/dandruff/`, `/fr/hydratation-cheveux-crepus/`, `/en/hydrate-type-4-hair/`.
- [ ] Set up **Bing Webmaster Tools** (import from Search Console).
- [ ] Confirm live: `schicgirl.me/ads.txt`, `/sitemap.xml`, `/fr/blog/` all load.

### Week 2 — Keyword check + Pinterest
- [ ] Validate your top 5 keywords in Google autocomplete + "People also ask" (KEYWORDS file).
- [ ] Create a **Pinterest business account**.
- [ ] Pin your ebook covers (link each pin to its product page, e.g. `/fr/pellicules/`).

### Week 3 — First new blog post
- [ ] Publish 1 post targeting an 🟢 easy keyword. *(I already built one as your example: "Transition without a big chop" — copy its structure.)*
- [ ] Share that post on Pinterest + your Instagram/Facebook.

### Week 4 — First backlinks
- [ ] Answer 3 real questions on Reddit (r/Naturalhair) / Facebook groups / Quora; link to a relevant page **only where genuinely helpful**.
- [ ] In Search Console → **Pages**: confirm your pages are getting indexed (green).

---

## MONTH 2 — Momentum (publish + promote)

### Week 5
- [ ] Publish blog post #2 (next easy keyword, e.g. "wash day routine for 4c hair").
- [ ] 5 Pinterest pins (mix covers + blog graphics).

### Week 6
- [ ] 3 more helpful community answers with links.
- [ ] Update 1 older page if Search Console shows it ranking position 8–20 (add a paragraph using that exact query).

### Week 7
- [ ] Publish blog post #3 (e.g. "hair porosity test at home" angle, or "how to moisturize 4c hair that stays dry").
- [ ] Pin it.

### Week 8
- [ ] Pitch 1 guest post or collaboration to a curly/coily-hair blog or French beauty page.
- [ ] Review Search Console **Performance** → note your top 3 queries so far.

---

## MONTH 3 — Compound (double down on what works)

### Week 9
- [ ] Publish blog post #4.
- [ ] Pinterest: keep ~3–5 pins/week (make it a habit).

### Week 10
- [ ] Find your "almost there" keywords (Search Console position 5–15) → expand those pages/posts with more depth.

### Week 11
- [ ] Publish blog post #5.
- [ ] 3 more community answers / 1 directory listing (natural-hair or Black-owned business).

### Week 12 — Review & plan next quarter
- [ ] Search Console review: which `/fr/` vs `/en/` pages grew? Which keywords moved up?
- [ ] Pick the 5 best-performing topics → plan the next 90 days around them.

---

### What "done right" looks like at day 90
You won't be #1 on big terms yet — that's normal. You SHOULD see: pages indexed,
first impressions/clicks in Search Console, a handful of long-tail keywords ranking
page 1–3, 5 new blog posts live, a growing Pinterest, and your first backlinks.
That's the launchpad. Keep the weekly rhythm and rankings keep climbing.

> The two habits that matter most, forever: **(1) publish one keyword-focused post
> every 1–2 weeks, (2) earn a few backlinks every week.** Everything else is detail.
