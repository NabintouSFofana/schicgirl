Build The Routine

Daily & Weekly Habits

Daily Habits Checklist
Start with water or a water-based mist — not oil
Seal damp hair with a light cream or oil
Refresh dry sections with a quick water spritz
Keep ends tucked or protected through the day
Re-moisturise only where needed — don’t soak
Hydrate yourself too: water + shield from sun & wind
Weekly Habits Checklist
Cleanse (shampoo or co-wash) to reset the hair
Deep condition — with heat if low porosity
Layer LOC or LCO right after wash day
Detangle gently on slip (conditioner or water)
Wear a protective or low-manipulation style
Light protein every 2–4 weeks if high porosity
Clarify every 2–4 weeks to clear buildup

Set Yourself Up

Products & Moisture Retention

Product Checklist — What Your Shelf Needs
A water-based leave-in (water = 1st ingredient)
A creamy moisturiser or butter
A sealing oil (jojoba, castor, grapeseed…)
A rich, moisturising deep conditioner
A light protein treatment (high porosity)
A gentle or clarifying cleanser
A spray bottle for water refreshes
Satin bonnet, scarf, or pillowcase
Moisture Retention Checklist — Keep It In
Always seal after you moisturise — never skip it
Layer in your porosity’s order: LOC (high) / LCO (low)
Style on damp hair, not soaking or bone-dry
Lock the ends — they dry first and break first
Sleep on satin every single night
Skip drying ingredients (harsh sulfates, drying alcohols)
Cool-rinse to close the cuticle (high porosity)

When Hair Is Dry Right Now

Emergency Dryness Checklist

1
Mist thoroughly with water until evenly damp
2
Smooth a leave-in (or a little conditioner) through
3
Seal with an oil, then a cream on the ends
4
Cover with a plastic cap 15–20 min (body heat)
5
Low porosity? Add gentle warmth to help it absorb
6
Loosely twist or braid, then let dry → soft, revived hair
7
Tonight: satin + a light water-and-oil refresh
Quick Wins This Week

Just three habits change everything: (1) start with water, (2) always seal, (3) sleep on satin. Do only these and you’ll feel the difference by your next wash day.

Remember This

Schicgirl Tips

  • Water is the only true moisturiser — everything else just helps it get in or stay in.
  • If you do one thing, seal. Moisture you don’t lock in is moisture you lose.
  • Match the method to porosity — LCO for low, LOC for high.
  • Treat your ends first and most — they’re the oldest, most fragile hair.
  • Consistency beats intensity — small daily habits outperform occasional rescue missions.
