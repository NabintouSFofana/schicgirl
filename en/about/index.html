Our mission

Making coily hair simple to understand

Schicgirl helps women with curly, kinky and coily hair — especially Type 4 (4A, 4B, 4C) — understand why their hair feels dry, build a simple routine that survives real life, and choose trusted products without overspending.

No jargon. No impossible 12-step routine. Just clear explanations, step-by-step plans and free tools so you can take back control of your hair.

My story

Hi, I'm Schicgirl 👋

I grew up believing my hair was just “too much.” Too dry, too difficult, too time-consuming. I tried every trending product, followed every viral routine — and my hair still kept breaking.

The turning point wasn't a miracle product. It came from understanding: porosity, moisture, sealing, gentle detangling. Once I understood the method, everything got simpler — and my hair finally grew healthy.
