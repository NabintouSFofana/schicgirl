Before you begin

Here's the honest promise, the same one we made before you joined: this challenge will reduce your breakage and give you a 5-minute routine that finally sticks. Less hair on wash day. Fewer snapped ends. A habit built around your real wash day.

Your first 3 steps

Begin today.

Gather your kit

Open the Workbook to the kit list. You don't need to buy anything new — a wide-tooth comb, your usual conditioner, water, and a few minutes. Lay it out so wash day is friction-free.

Take your Day-1 baseline

Snap your starting photos and run the Day-1 comb test exactly as the Tracker shows. This is your "before." You can't see progress you never measured — so don't skip it.

Watch for the Day-7 check

Your first real comparison is the Day-7 wash-day check. Log your wash-day breakage in the Tracker, then compare the pile to Day 1. That shrinking pile is your proof it's working.
